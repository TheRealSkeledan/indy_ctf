About IndyCTF

Welcome to IndyCTF — Independence High School’s entry-level, beginner-friendly Capture The Flag event designed to help new students try cybersecurity without the pressure. If you’ve ever wanted to dip your toes into CTFs but felt intimidated, this is the perfect place to start.

The Challenge

COMING SOON

The specific categories, point breakdowns, and challenge details will be revealed at the start of the competition. Expect a mix of classic CTF styles: forensics, OSINT, cryptography, web, steganography, and more. No heavy coding or Linux-based terminal knowledge required — we keep it accessible.

What to Expect

  • You'll get a clear set of challenges and point values when the event begins.
  • You'll work in teams of up to four to solve puzzles across different cybersecurity categories.
  • All challenges are designed to help you learn, not overwhelm you — perfect for first-timers.
  • No programming or terminal-heavy tasks. Everything is doable with guided reasoning and tools provided.
  • The environment is supportive, relaxed, and meant to build confidence.

What to Bring

  • Your computer, charger, and the ability to run a virtual machine.
  • Any notes or references you find helpful.
  • An open mind and willingness to try something new.

Prizes

Prizes are TBD, but expect them to be legitimately good — not the usual “sticker and handshake” type. Every team walks away with something, but top teams will get the best rewards.

Why Join IndyCTF?

  • A low-stress entry point into cybersecurity competitions.
  • A chance to experience problem-solving under time pressure without any real risk.
  • Team-based learning that helps you build confidence fast.
  • Great experience for resumes, college apps, and anyone curious about cybersecurity.
  • It’s actually fun. You’ll surprise yourself with what you can solve.

Our Vision

IndyCTF exists to give new students a safe space to learn, experiment, and fail without judgment. Cybersecurity shouldn’t feel gatekept — this event is built to tear down that barrier and help you find your footing in a community that welcomes curiosity.

FAQ

What is a CTF?

A Capture The Flag competition is a set of cybersecurity puzzles you solve to earn points. It’s hands-on learning disguised as a competition.

Do I need experience?

No. IndyCTF was literally made for people with zero experience.

Who can compete?

Anyone at Independence who wants to try cybersecurity, learn, or compete for prizes.

How big are teams?

Up to 4 people. Bring a full squad or join one when you arrive.

Is AI allowed?

Yes, you can use AI tools for brainstorming and assistance. Just don’t expect them to solve everything for you.

What skills will I learn?

How to approach unfamiliar problems, break puzzles apart, manage time in a competitive environment, and get comfortable with cybersecurity basics.

Get Ready

IndyCTF is your chance to test your curiosity, learn fast, and have fun with no pressure. The full challenge set drops at the event. Come ready to jump in.
