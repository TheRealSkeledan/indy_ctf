WHY SPONSOR INDY CTF?

INDY CTF is our school's first-ever Capture The Flag competition, bringing together students passionate about cybersecurity. By sponsoring our event, you help foster the next generation of security professionals while gaining visibility among motivated learners and local tech enthusiasts.

Your support directly enables us to provide challenges, prizes, resources, and a memorable learning experience for all participants. This is an opportunity to connect with emerging talent and show your commitment to education in our community.
